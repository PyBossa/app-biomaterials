Why?

As researchers, we are developing new materials for targeted drug delivery and tissue engineering. We want to cure cancers, treat osteoarthritis, and fabricate new tissues!

How?

We engineer materials comprised of proteins via genetic engineering and molecular biology. While we use structural elements found in natural proteins, we piece them in ways not found in nature. In doing so, we create new protein materials.

How you can help

These materials assemble into nanoparticles that we need your help in characterizing. Features such as size and shape and numbers are critical to the overall properties of the materials. You will help us achieve characterization of these important features.

How your help will make a difference?

Knowing the properties mentioned above, we can relate them directly back to the proteins materials. You are helping us to get closer to curing cancers, treating osteoarthritis, and fabricating new tissues!
